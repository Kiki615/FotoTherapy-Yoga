What is FotoTherapy Yoga

FotoTherapy Yoga is a revolutionary new approach to practicing yoga. Combining light therapy and color psychology techniques, brain stimulating soundscapes and bespoke natural scents to create an immersive, multi-sensory yoga experience not yet seen before. Based in Nashville, Tennessee, the FotoYoga studio is a one of a kind space, cut off from the outside world which transforms as it moves through the colours of the spectrum.

Blue Light to Sanitize Spaces

Eliminate up to 99% of allergens, viruses, bacteria, and mold.

Red Light to Heal Muscles

Increases blood circulation and flow to help muscle tissue regenerate and heal.

Green Light to Improve Skin Texture
